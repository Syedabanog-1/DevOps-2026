# 📝 Week 20 - Day 1: Helm and K8s Incident Response

## 🎯 What is it?
Helm is a package manager that makes installing complex Kubernetes apps as easy as 'helm install'.

**Analogy:** Think of it like **An App Store for Kubernetes**.

## 💡 Why do we care?
In modern DevOps, everything must be automated, scalable, and reliable. Mastering Helm and K8s Incident Response allows you to build systems that work without human intervention and scale globally.

## 🛠 Basic Concepts
- **Automation**: Let the computer do the repetitive work.
- **Reliability**: If it breaks, it should recover automatically.
- **Traceability**: You should always know *what* changed, *when*, and *why*.

## 🧠 Best Practices
- Keep it simple and readable.
- Always test locally before deploying to production.
- Document your configurations clearly.
