# 📝 Week 33 - Day 1: DevSecOps and Scanning

## 🎯 What is it?
Integrating security checks (SAST/DAST) into every step of the development process.

**Analogy:** Think of it like **Security guards checking IDs**.

## 💡 Why do we care?
In modern DevOps, everything must be automated, scalable, and reliable. Mastering DevSecOps and Scanning allows you to build systems that work without human intervention and scale globally.

## 🛠 Basic Concepts
- **Automation**: Let the computer do the repetitive work.
- **Reliability**: If it breaks, it should recover automatically.
- **Traceability**: You should always know *what* changed, *when*, and *why*.

## 🧠 Best Practices
- Keep it simple and readable.
- Always test locally before deploying to production.
- Document your configurations clearly.
