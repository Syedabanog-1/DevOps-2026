# 📝 Week 32 - Day 1: Alerting and On-Call

## 🎯 What is it?
AlertManager pages you when something breaks, ensuring issues are addressed immediately.

**Analogy:** Think of it like **A fire alarm system**.

## 💡 Why do we care?
In modern DevOps, everything must be automated, scalable, and reliable. Mastering Alerting and On-Call allows you to build systems that work without human intervention and scale globally.

## 🛠 Basic Concepts
- **Automation**: Let the computer do the repetitive work.
- **Reliability**: If it breaks, it should recover automatically.
- **Traceability**: You should always know *what* changed, *when*, and *why*.

## 🧠 Best Practices
- Keep it simple and readable.
- Always test locally before deploying to production.
- Document your configurations clearly.
